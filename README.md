# D2C Skincare Brand Growth Strategy Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the performance of a Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) skincare brand and provides data-driven recommendations to support its goal of becoming a leader in the skincare segment.

The analysis focuses on customer acquisition, retention, sales performance, product portfolio, customer experience, and growth opportunities using business intelligence and data analytics techniques.

---

## Business Objective

To identify strategic actions that can help a D2C skincare brand:

* Improve growth quality
* Strengthen product portfolio leadership
* Enhance customer retention and customer experience
* Drive sustainable long-term growth

## Key Insights

* Revenue remained healthy throughout the analysis period, indicating stable customer demand.
* Instagram emerged as the strongest customer acquisition channel.
* The Website generated the highest revenue and order volume.
* UPI was the most preferred payment method.
* Serum was identified as the hero product category.
* Repeat customers contributed significantly to revenue, highlighting strong retention potential.
* Product returns revealed customer experience improvement opportunities.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Growth Engine Prioritization

Focus investment on high-performing acquisition channels and strengthen customer retention programs.

### Portfolio Leadership

Expand successful product categories and continue innovation around hero products.

### Customer Experience Excellence

Reduce return-related friction through improved product communication, fulfillment, and customer support.
